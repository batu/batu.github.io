Thesis: Robolution

My thesis project aims to create a self balancing competitive game. The basic idea is that balancing competitive games is a very slow and tedious process. So instead I am trying to automate this process using Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence . The game will be constantly recording data from each individual play session and make incremental balance changes based on the user data. This hopefully will allow me to create a more active meta game. I am also planning on evolving non player characther artificial intelligences using neural networks to get more data. Learn more about this project in this presentation or in this proposal.

MiniDungeons

Eco Eco is an Android game made by Unity3D . The game aims to increase public awareness on global warming by giving the players a habitat to manage by putting plants and animals. The way players unlock assets is by playing real life mini games such as walking a certain distance or taking a picture of a reusable water bottle. The app itself uses Moves and CamFind API's in its back end.

Hello!

Game Recommend is a website that allows you to find games to your liking. You start by selecting your favourite game. The system then presents similar games to you. If you click on the one that interests you, you get three more games! You can leap from one interesting game to another until you find one that you really want to play. After that, you can save this path so that other people can trace your interest steps. The project has a NodeJS and Express back end and uses the GiantBomb API.

Jeremy Cohen

Jeremy Cohen is a dungeon crawler made by Unity3D . The player has the option to stop time and que actions in order to execute them in a satisfying flurry of devastating blows. The player customizes his play style as he goes through procedurally generated levels. C# and Maya are some of the technologies we used to create this game.

ID Hero

In the situation when a government is unstable, corrupt or non-existent, verifying one's identity to access financial services becomes a challenging problem. IDHero substitutes the role of central authority in providing ID, and permits users to claim their identity via the social networks of friends, family and co-workers by taking advantage of an SMS based service.

The Great Tagalyzer

The Tagalyzer is a web application that runs Map Reduce jobs on Amazon Web Services . to parse through tens thousands of tweets on a given hashtags. Then the app calcualtes the average sentiment value to decide what the general public feels about the given hashtag.

My Reflection

A web app that allows you to see the sentiment value of your tweets before you send them off to the wild. We hope that this will result in a moment of reflection and thus make the internet a less toxic place. The application is built using Express and Cordova .
