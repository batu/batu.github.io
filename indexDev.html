Projects

Jeremy Cohen

Jeremy Cohen is a dungeon crawler made by Unity3D. The player has the option to stop time and que actions in order to execute them in a satisfying flurry of devestating blows. The player customizes his play style as he goes through procedurally generated levels. C# and Maya are some of the technologies we used to create this game.

Eco Eco

Eco Eco is a android game made by Unity3D. The game aims to increase public awareness on global warming by giving the players a habitat to manage by putting plants and animals. The way players unlock assets is by playing real life mini games such as walking a certain distance or taking a picture of a reusable water bottle.

Game Recommendation Engine

Game Recommend is a website that allows you to find games to your liking. You start by selecting your favourite game. The system then presents to you similar games. If you click on the one that interests you you get three more games! This allows you to leap from interesting game to game untill you find one that you really want to play. After that you can save this path so that other people can trace your interest steps. The project has a NodeJS back end and uses the GiantBomb API.
